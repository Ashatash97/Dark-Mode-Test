Welcome to the Library of Influential Asian American Women


The Library of Asian American Women serves to be a public resource to those curious about the Asian American Women who have had and continue to have an impact throughout our history and the modern day. This library seeks to be a documentation of names and biographies of Asian American women across all backgrounds and types of achievements; in one accessable center. Both an educational resource and interactive art piece, the Library of Asian American Women hopes to bring inspiration and joy to new generations.
